Lisa — quick on the earnest money question. Short answer: 1–2% of offer price, due 1–2 days after acceptance, held in the listing brokerage's escrow. On a $450K offer that's ~$4,500–9,000 — and it credits to your down payment at closing. Couple things to know past the basics: ...

What changed

  • Opens with the answer (1–2%, 1–2 days, escrow). No greeting delay.
  • "Quick on the…" is a phrase from your exemplars (used 11x in your last 90 days of texts).
  • Specific numbers tied to her actual price band.
  • One natural transition ("Couple things to know past the basics…") instead of a corporate one.
What the voice guide remembers: Your voice was characterized in onboarding as "knowledgeable older sibling explaining a thing they actually understand." Effusive openings break that the fastest.

What happens now

The original draft is held until rewritten. Marcus Voice runs automatically on every other helper's output — you almost never invoke it directly. When you do, it's because something feels off and you want a second read.
