4502 N. Hermitage — marketing package

MLS description · Version A (warm)

A 2019 reno that held up — and the block to match. 4502 N. Hermitage sits on the school-side stretch of Hermitage, where the pace slows and the western light pours into the kitchen all afternoon. Three bedrooms, two-and-a-half baths, 2,180 sqft of thoughtfully renovated single-family living, with a primary suite and a finished lower level that actually gets used. The kitchen is the room buyers will remember: white oak cabinets, soapstone counters, double pantry, and the kind of light that makes mornings feel slower than they are. The dining and living rooms keep the original 1908 bones — restored trim, tall ceilings, a fireplace that works. Outside: a private fenced yard, two-car garage, and a front porch that gets used. Walk to Lawrence Brown Line, six minutes to Lincoln Square's restaurants, four blocks to Ravenswood Manor's parks. The block is the rest of the story.

MLS description · Version B (tight)

2019-renovated three-bedroom single-family in Ravenswood, on a quiet block of Hermitage near Lincoln Square. Soapstone-and-white-oak kitchen, primary suite, finished lower level, fenced yard, two-car garage. Two-and-a-half baths. 2,180 sqft. Walk to Lawrence Brown Line and Ravenswood Manor parks. Original 1908 detail preserved through the reno.

Recommend Version A. The block-level specificity is consistent with how you write — and it gives buyer agents a story to repeat.
