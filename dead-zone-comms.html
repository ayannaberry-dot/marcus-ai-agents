Reilly check-in · 17 days since last property tour

Pulled context: Reillys are at $625K, looking in Lakeview / Roscoe Village. Inventory in their range has been thin (2 new fits in 17 days, both flawed).

Draft message

To: Tom & Anne Reilly · Channel: Email
From: Marcus Mason
Tom and Anne — Quick check-in. I know it's been quiet on your end. I want you to know what's actually been happening behind the scenes: • Inventory in your range has been thin. Two new listings hit MLS in the last 17 days that fit your bedroom count and price band — one on Henderson, one on Wolfram. I passed on both. The Henderson one had a tandem parking situation that doesn't fit your lifestyle; the Wolfram one was overpriced 8% on the comps and I think it'll sit. Watching both for price drops. • I've talked to three buyer agents in your zip code this week about coming-soon listings. One has a 3-bd on Roscoe at $639K coming live next Wednesday. I'm getting you in for a private showing before the open house if I can. • The Lake View East quietly added 4 listings this week below $700K. I haven't pushed them to you because none are great — but if we want to broaden our search to include LVE for the right block, I think there's a real shot in the next 30 days. The market is genuinely thin right now in Lakeview proper. I'd rather wait for the right thing than push you to write on something that's "fine." Talk Friday and I'll have the Roscoe details. — Marcus

Why this works

  • Specific: Names addresses, names buyer agents, names the kind of work you've been doing. Not "we're working on it."
  • Honest: Doesn't pretend the market is hotter than it is. The Reillys will appreciate this more than spin.
  • Forward: Roscoe lead, possible LVE pivot. Gives them something to react to.
Why this matters: The April 17 client (Carla) was lost in exactly this kind of dead zone. She felt she was searching alone. The fix isn't more activity — it's making the activity visible.

What happens now

You review and send. The Reilly client file logs the check-in. If they don't reply within 4 days, it auto-flags for a follow-up nudge (which you also send). If they engage, the Roscoe showing kicks off the buyer-side flow.
